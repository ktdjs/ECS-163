From Cell Types to Molecules:
A Multi-Scale View of the Human Transcriptome

This visualization explores gene expression across five scales of biological organization — from a population of blood cells down to the nucleotide sequence of a single gene. Start broad (cell type populations), then drill into the cell, its compartments, its pathways, and finally an individual gene. Each level uses a distinct visual vocabulary matched to its data type.
